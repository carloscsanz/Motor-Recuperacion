x1900xt 3D Shooters performance review

Battlefield 2 benchmark

The performance of the x1900xtx are very similar to the 7900gtx. borth card provide enough fps to play the game with with higherst setting 1600/1200 and maintain good performance

The x1900xtx leave the lead on this test it get beaten by over 20 fps at 1024/786 resolution with is quite a lot .  but the card still provding enough fps for good game play. The game is based on opengl and and stencil shadows . this may explique the nvidia lead .

Doom 3 benchmark

another opengl game. doom has alwais been better performer with nvidia card . also we should note the nvidia driver witch are more optimized for open gl application. the x1900xtx has been beaten by less than 10 % . the average frame rate is pretty similar for nvidia 7900gtx card

Far Cry benchmark

and finally here the x1900xtx take the lead.. note that the geforce nv 7 gpu do not support Antialiasing and hdr . The x1900xtx loose with higher resolution . the SM3.0 is implemented in far cry so there is nice boost in the performance for nvidia card

x1900xtx average here is about 10 to 15 fps lower than the nvdia 7900gtx card witch about 15% of performane lost.nvdida card 7900gtx still in the lead in average of all previews benchmark

FEAR benchmark

This game play Despite the huge number of complex shader-based special effects

The x1900xt  get the lead here only in 1600/1200 resolution but the frame rate is somewhere between 25/51 fps so it is pretty low for such high end card . the 1024/786 is optimal resoltuion for those card performance to enjoy .or u can turn of the Antialiasing for better frame rate at the 1600 / 1200 level

Quake 4 performance

Despite full-screen antialiasing and OpenGL, the Gigabyte Radeon X1900 XTX and Gigabyte GeForce 7900 GTX have the same performance in a normal gaming situation with lots of shots and action. Their average performance is over 100fps in all the resolutions. The minimum of speed is quite high, so you wonвЂ™t be disappointed when playing Quake 4 on a top-end graphics card.

x1900xtx The Elder Scrolls IV: Oblivion performance

In dungeons and closed environments in general it is important that the graphics card be able to effectively process pixel shaders as well as lighting and shadowing. The advantages of the Radeon X1900 architecture show up in a rather peculiar way here: the average performance of the Gigabyte Radeon X1900 XTX differs but slightly from that of the Gigabyte GeForce 7900 GTX, but the Radeon has a considerably higher minimum of speed! It means the game is going to run smoother on the ATI-based card, giving you more playing comfort.

Considering Oblivionis not all about closed environments, we recommend you using a resolution lower than 1600x1200 even on flagships products from Nvidia and ATI to safeguard yourself against slowdowns.

x1900xt review conclusion

The x1900xtx is solid card but I t is usually beaten by the 7900gtx.

In particular, Gigabyte Radeon x1900 XTX demonstrates excellent performance in applications that require high shader processing speed and deal with complex mathematical calculations, but gives in in tasks with high fillrate requirements. Among its major advantages we should certainly mention 48 pixel processors and extremely efficient memory subsystem with ring bus memory controller as well as simultaneous support of FSAA and HDR (FP16).
