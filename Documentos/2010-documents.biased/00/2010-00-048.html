Spike TV Video Game Awards Recap
December 13th, 2009 at 3:08 am -

Here is a quick rundown of the goings on at the VGAs for all those who either couldn’t see it or may have missed some.

Several surprises came from tonight’s Spike TV Video Game Awards including,

  • Batman Arkham Asylum Sequel announcement
  • True Crime announcement
  • Star Wars: The Force Unleashed II announcement
  • Reveal Trailer for Spec Ops: The Line
  • Green Day Rock Band

The Winners were,

  • Best Voice: Jack Black/ Eddie Riggs
  • Best Action Adventure Game: Assassins Creed II
  • Best Studio: Rocksteady
  • Best Team Sports Game: NHL 10
  • Best Performance by a Human Male: Hugh Jakcman
  • Best Cast: X-Men Origins: Wolverine
  • Best Indie Game: Flower
  • Best Individual Sports Game: UFC Undisputed
  • Best RPG: Dragon Age: Origins
  • Best PC Game: Dragon Age: Origins
  • Best Performance by a Human Female: Megan Fox
  • Best Downloadable Game: Shadow Complex
  • Best Wii Game: New Super Mario Bros. Wii
  • Most Anticipated Game: God of War III
  • Best Shooter: Modern Warfare 2
  • Best Multiplayer: Modern Warfare 2
  • Best Fighter: Street Fighter 4
  • Best Handheld Game: Grand Theft Auto Chinatown Wars
  • Best Driving Game: Forza 3
  • Best Soundtrack: DJ Hero
  • Best Xbox 360 Game: Left 4 Dead 2
  • Best Original Score: Halo 3: ODST
  • Best Game Based on Movie/TV Show: South Park Let’s Go Tower Defense Play
  • Best Music Game: The Beatles Rock Band
  • Best Playstation 3 Game: Uncharted 2
  • Best Graphics: Uncharted 2
  • Game of the Year: Uncharted 2

A few final thoughts about the show include,

  • Oh my god did you see Halo Reach?
  • Seriously did you see Halo Reach it looked fantastic.
  • Why won’t they announce the Beta for Halo Reach already?

Well that’s what went down in tonight’s Video Game Awards. Speaking of awards, keep it here on Gaming Bolt for some awards of our own coming soon.
