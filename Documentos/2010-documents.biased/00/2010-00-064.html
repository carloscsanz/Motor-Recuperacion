Video Game Award’s 2009 Results

8 comments

Bear in mind this is the best I can do just under a day after the VGA`s. Viacom are assholes.

Best Voice
My Vote: Jack Black as Eddie Riggs (Brutal Legend)
Winner: Jack Black As Eddie Riggs

Best Action Adventure Game
My Vote: Brutal Legend
Winner: Assassin’s Creed II

Studio Of The Year
My Vote: Infinity Ward or Naughty Dog
Winner: Rocksteady

Best Team Sports Game
My Vote: NHL 10
Winner: NHL 10

Best Performance By A Human Male
My Vote: Billy Murray as Dr. Peter Venkman (Ghostbusters)
Winner: Hugh Jackman as Wolverine (X-Men Origins)

Best Cast
My Vote: Brutal Legend
Winner: X-Men Origins

Best Independent Game Fueled By Dew
My Vote: Trails HD
Winner: Flower

Best Individual Sports Game
My Vote: UFC 2009 Undisputed
Winner: UFC 2009 Undisputed

Best RPG
My Vote: Borderlands
Winner: Dragon Age: Origins

Best PC Game
My Vote: Sims 3
Winner: Dragon Age: Origins

Best Wii Game
My Vote: New Super Mario Bros.
Winner: New Super Mario Bros

Best Performance By A Human Female
My Vote: Eliza Duhsku as Rubi (WET)
Winner: Meghan Fox

Best Downloadable Game
My Vote: Shadow Complex
Winner: Shadow Complex

Best DLC
My Vote: The Ballad of Gay Tony (GTA4)
Winner: The Ballad of Gay Tony (GTA4)

Most Anticipated Game
My Vote (Own Submission): Heavy Rain
Winner: God Of War 3

Best Shooter
My Vote: Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2
Winner: Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2

Best Multiplayer Game
My Vote: Borderlands
Winner: Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2

Best Games Based On A Movie/TV Show
My Vote: South Park Let’s Go Tower Defense Play
Winner: South Park Let’s Go Tower Defense Play

Best XBOX 360 Game
My Vote: Left 4 Dead 2
Winner: Left 4 Dead 2

Best Soundtrack
My Vote: Brutal Legend
Winner: DJ Hero

Best Handheld Game
My Vote: GTA: Chinatown Wars
Winner: GTA: Chinatown Wars

Best Fighting Game
My Vote: Street Fighter 4
Winner: Street Fighter 4

Best Driving Game
My Vote: NFS Shift
Winner: Forza 3

Best Original Score
My Vote: CoD: Modern Warfare 2
Winner: Halo 3: ODST

Best Music Game
My Vote: The Beatles Rock Band
Winner: The Beatles Rock Band

Best PS3 Game
My Vote: Uncharted 2: Among Thieves
Winner: Uncharted 2: Among Thieves

Best Graphics
My Vote: Uncharted 2: Among Thieves
Winner: Uncharted 2: Among Thieves

Game Of The Year
My Vote: Call Of Duty: Modern Warfare 2 or Uncharted 2: Among Thieves
Winner: Uncharted 2: Among Thieves

Studio of the Year was a shock, Naughty Dog or Infinity Ward should have been the winner. Best Original Score should have went to Hans Zimmer for CoD:MW2. Best Driving Game should have went to NFS Shift, it just looked better than Forza 3. Best Soundtarck and Cast SHOULD have went to Brutal Legend. With a cast of Rock Gods and an all metal soundtrack the VGA`s disappointed me in that winner.
