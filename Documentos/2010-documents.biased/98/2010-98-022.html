Spike 2008 Video Game Award results
Lydia Sung - Monday, December 15th, 2008 | 2:12PM (PT) 0 Favourites (0)

In case any of you were wondering...

Ah yes, award shows.  Whether you love or hate them, most can't deny a gnawing curiosity that tempts us to glimpse at the results just to see if our personal favorites earned a place among the winning roster.

Tears were shed following G4's X-Play awards, and slap fights were instigated before SpikeTV held their own VGA event.  Here are the results from last night's VGA awards show, just to clarify any lingering doubts.

Best Independent Game World of Goo

Studio of the Year Media Molecule 

Game of the Year Grand Theft Auto IV

Best Team Sports Game NHL 09

Big Name in the Game Female Jenny McCarthy, C&C: Red Alert 3

Big Name in the Game Male Kiefer Sutherland, CoD: World at War

Best Performance by a Human Female Debbie Mae West, MGS4

Best Performance by a Human Male Michael Hollick, GTA IV

Best Multi-Player Game Left 4 Dead

Best Original Score Metal Gear Solid 4

Best PC Game Left 4 Dead

Best PS3 Game LittleBigPlanet

Best Wii Game Boom Blox

Best Xbox 360 Game Gears of War 2

Best Soundtrack Rock Band 2

Best Action Adventure Game Grand Theft Auto IV

Best Driving Game Burnout Paradise

Best Music Game Rock Band 2

Best Game Based on a Movie or TV Show LEGO Indiana Jones

Best Graphics Metal Gear Solid 4

Best Handheld Game Professor Layton and the Curious Village

Best Individual Sports Game Shaun White Snowboarding

Best Fighting Game Soul Calibur IV

Best RPG Fallout 3

Best Shooter Gears of War 2

Most Anticipated Game Grand Theft Auto IV

Source: SPIKE 2008 Video Game Awards
