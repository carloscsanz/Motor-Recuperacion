Spike TV’s Video Game Awards 2009 Recap

Monday, December 14, 2009

Held this past Saturday, Spike TV’s Video Games Awards showcased the latest and greatest of 2009. Utilizing fan based voting, gamers could hop on Spike TV’s website and vote for their favorite games that have hit this year. Although awards were a main showing, Spike TV promised gamers a night  they would never forget with over 6 new reveals occurring as well. In case you didn’t get a chance to watch it (like me, at work) we have the breakdown of awards as follows:

Awards
