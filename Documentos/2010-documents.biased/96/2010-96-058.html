News: Videogame Awards 2009

By
Jassi Patayon
Published: December 13, 2009Posted in: General, NewsTags: 2009, Awards, Game, Spike, vga, Video
  • Comments [4]
  • Digg it!
  • Facebook

It’s been 6 years since Spike TV aired its VGA (Videogame Awards) ceremony. Since then, the production has become bigger, better, and much more elaborate.  This year, for the first time, Spike’s VGAs went global to show the world the games that deserve the awards in 2009.

Nice isn't it?

In 2007, Samuel L. Jackson was the honorary host of the awards. Last year we had Jack Black take the stage as the Master of Ceremonies. This year we have….well nobody. This year, the VGAs did not have a specific host.  Instead, a lot of stars and famous nick-nackers presented games and awards to the people on the other side of the telly (Television in UK). So, forget huge stars on the stage being hosts, because this year, it’s all about the games.

This year, the VGAs went heavy on the game and movie trailers that are coming up in 2010 and 2011 with what seemed like more trailers and announcements then actual awards. Many sequels, reboots of old franchises, and other goodies filled the 2 hour event. Here is a list of movies and games unveiled during the ceremony:

- Prince of Persia: Sands of Time

With all of these announcements, you may have forgotten about the awards? Well here’s the list for the games that won the awards this year, and again CONGRATULATIONS to the developers who created these phenomenal masterpieces!


- GAME OF THE YEAR: Uncharted 2: Among Thieves

GAME OF THE YEAR

- Best Voice for a Videogame: Jack Black as Eddie Riggs in Brutal Legends

- Best Action-Adventure: Assassin’s Creed II

- Studio of the Year: Rocksteady Studios for Batman: Arkham Assylum

- Best Team Sport: NHL 10

Best Team Sports

- Best Male Performance: Hugh Jackman as Wolverine in X-men: Wolverine

- Best Female Performance: Megan Fox as Mikaela Banes in Transformers 2: Rise of the Fallen

- Best Cast: Hugh Jackman as Wolverine

- Best Independent Game: Flower

- Best Individual Sports: UFC Undisputed 2009

- Best RPG: Dragon Age: Origins

Best RPG & PC Game

- Best PC Game: Dragon Age: Origins

- Best Downloadable Game: Shadow Complex

- Best Wii Game: New Super Mario Bros.

Best Wii Game

- Best Downloadable Content: Grand Theft Auto 4: Ballad of Gay Tony

- Most Anticipated Game: God of War 3

- Best Shooter: Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2

Best Shooter & Multiplayer

- Best Multiplayer: Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2

- Best Fighting Game: Street Fighter 4

- Best Handheld Game: Grand Theft Auto: Chinatown Wars

- Best Driving Game: Forza Motorsport 3

- Best Soundtrack: DJ Hero

- Best Xbox 360 Game:  Left 4 Dead 2

Best Xbox 360 game

- Best Original Score: Halo: ODST

- Best Game Based on a Show: South Park: Let’s Go Tower Defense

- Best Music:  Rockband: Beatles

Best Music

- Best PS3 Game: Uncharted 2: Among Thieves

- Best Graphics: Uncharted 2: Among Thieves
