SIGIR Financial Outlook The Good News
  • Recent increase in membership
  • Is it sustainable?
  • Growth in SIGIR Proceedings Package
  • But we dont make money on it
  • Conferences generally profitable
  • Usually small profits but highly variable
  • Large fund balance

9
SIGIR Financial Outlook The Bad News
  • Declining sales of SIGIR proceedings
  • Declining interest income
  • Increasing ACM costs
  • Increasing student travel other costs
  • A good thing but how do we pay for it?
  • Unpredictable conference income
  • Exec Committee has had little control

10
SIGIR Financial Outlook Assessment
  • SIGIR is healthy financially but
  • Dues cover less than half our expenses
  • We rely on conference profits to fund services
    e.g. student travel awards
  • Conference profits and losses vary greatly
  • Options
  • Exec Committee assumes greater control over
    conference finances
  • Raise membership dues
  • Raise publication prices
