Battlefield 2 is a game focused on modern war. This game that allows online battles between 64 players (32x32).

Battlefield 2 added a big improvement in the graphics of the game and a game engine that much better than the previous ones, however only enjoys the excellent graphics that the game has to offer if they have a good computer.

The basis of the game is still the same, however unlike Battlefield 1942 in which there were more modes of play, Battlefield 2 only has the Conquest mode, a mode where to win the game is necessary to win the flags and advocates them. Another of the great innovations of this game is the way to commander (captain), in which the player who hold this position during the game will have access to a menu where you can view the whole of the battlefield, knowing where they are allied troops and the enemy , which is a great advantage.

This game also adds a system of rankings in which the players who play on ranked servers and meet certain objectives (eg, kill 7 enemies of a knife round, or heal 8 elements of team) win medals and other extras that will be in stats of the player and can be accessed through the menu of the game or on sites that offer. One of the major defects of the game is to delay the download of the maps.

Unlike other fps in which there is a single player with a good story and that many of the times many of the players are single player in the series battlefield the singleplayer serves only to those who want to explore a little the maps that exist or to test some something, because as has been customary, the match of the series battlefield have a very singleplayer crush, and the game essentially turned to the online component, where the game reaches all its splendor. Being a game of this team, the work of team is very important, which can only be achieved by clans and is almost impossible to achieve public servants have any organization (few exceptions).


The gameplay is not complicated and is easy to learn the buttons after some little time.

On the sound, Battlefield 2 is not as strong as its predecessor Battlefield Vietnam which we provided a list of songs in game. In Battlefield 2 the music is always the same, there is an option to take the music as is the loading of a map, which can become a little annoying.

Battlefield 2 is an evolution in the series, with its new graphic engine and the innovation can still go much further. The essence of the series remains and therefore the fans of the series and for those who like modern war this is an excellent game. The Battlefield series is more arcade than real, so the players who like realism, we may not like much of the series BF, however, this does not mean that this was not a great game and did not provide many hours of fun, which may be extended if it is a clan. In this game did not kill anyone with a shot, few exceptions, so those who like realism of games as AA (americas army) will be the ideas. Anyway, who buy the game is not going to repent and have guaranteed many hours of fun.

I played BF2 at a local computer gaming cafe last year and I tell you it's awesome! It's the best online game I've ever played since Counter-Strike.

I've enjoyed playing all the sides (US, China, Middle East Coalition) and almost all the units (except for engineer) and I love riding the various vehicles in the maps. The last time I played it, I was somewhere between Major and Lieutenant and I had some upgrade weapons. I recently bought the Special Forces expansion pack. Unfortunately, I don't have a powerful computer to run Battlefield 2, so I'm currently saving money to buy a new one so I can play BF2.

Overall, I loved it. I prefer BF2's realism and strategy over the other otherwise over-the-top online shooters relying on sci-fi weaponry and mayhem. Highly recommend for shooting fans who wanted a T-rated alternative to the many mature action games out there

THE PROS:
- Balanced units and weapons
- Great vehicle control
- Intense firefights
- Highly detailed graphics
- Multiplayer maps
- Highly-detailed graphics
- Strategic and team-based elements

THE CONS:
- Some server hiccups
- Glitchy loading times

OVERALL - 4.5 out of 5
