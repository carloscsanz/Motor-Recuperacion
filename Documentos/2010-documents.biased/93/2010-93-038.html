Yes, I know this thread is kinda along the lines of the "post your specs" thread, but I think it's different enough to be a seperate thread. And yes, there are threads already on 3DMark06, but I'd rather not hijack one of those threads as they are discussing the program rather than a thread to post scores in.

Post your 3DMark scores here. If you don't have 3DMark / know what it is, it's a utility to test the performance of your computer, mainly your video card.

You can download the free versions here:

* 3DMark06 -- http://www.futuremark.com/download/?3dmark06.shtml
This is the latest version that came out today. It's designed for the latest and greatest systems and video cards. Expect to get a low score on this.

* 3DMark05 -- http://www.futuremark.com/download/?3dmark05.shtml
The version that's probably best fitting for most systems here. It's still not an easy test, but at least you can get a decent score.

* 3DMark03 -- http://www.futuremark.com/download/?3dmark03.shtml
This version is starting to show it's age and unless you have a crappy system, expect to get a pretty good score.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

I'll start with my aging system:

* 3DMark06: 2379 -- http://service.futuremark.com/compare?3dm06=11841
Stock CPU, heavily overclocked video card. It was artifacting a bit on the forest level, but oh well.

* 3DMark05: 4093 -- http://service.futuremark.com/compare?3dm05=1659971
Ehh, I'm still working on this one. This score with from all stock speeds. However, with an overclocked CPU and GPU, I scored 5429, but the overclocking of my CPU caused my RAM timings to go to shit, so I dropped the CPU overclocking. Haven't run it with stock CPU / overclocked video card yet since I got my 2nd gig of RAM.

* 3DMark03: 11252 -- http://service.futuremark.com/compare?2k3=4529022
Haven't run it again since I got my 2nd gig of RAM, but I doubt it'd change it much, if at all. Stock CPU and video card speeds, so I need to run it again with an overclocked video card.
