Re:New Student Discount Website launched 3 Weeks, 5 Days ago  
Have you got a facebook and twitter page? Facebook is more important than Twitter to get up and running but if you're reaching out to students then Facebook is a MUST! Link your Facebook and Twitter pages to your website to get more traffic visiting your site. College students live on Facebook and Youtube. I was on your site and I couldn't see any Facebook link so get your Facebook page up and running asap! It's probably the best way you could promote to Students - and better still - it's FREE! Just create your Business Page, go to the search bar, enter UCD, DIT etc, join the college groups, add all of the members of the college groups, and start posting all of your great offers :)
