Convert MP3, WAV, WMA, OGG and VOX files from one to another

December, 2003

AliveMedia has released Alive MP3 WAV Converter 1.6.3.8, an all-in-one audio converter for the Windows 98/NT/ME/2K/XP platforms, that can convert MP3, WAV, WMA, OGG and VOX files from one to another with a single right-click, quick and easy.

Alive MP3 WAV Converter lets you to convert all popular audio formats including MP3(MPEG Layer-3, including VBR), WAV(PCM), WMA(Windows Media Audio 9), OGG(Ogg vorbis), VOX(Dialogic ADPCM). You can also convert MPC, AVI, MP1, MP2, MPA, g721, g726, g723 or RAW files to MP3, WAV, OGG, WMA and VOX files. In addition, Alive MP3 WAV Converter also allows you to normalize your MP3, WMA, OGG, WAV or VOX files with a single right-click.

The intuitive interface makes Alive MP3 WAV Converter the perfect tool for both new and experienced users. Just right click on files in the Windows Explorer, and select "Convert to MP3"(or WAV, OGG, WMA, VOX) to convert them. It fully supports command line and schedule converting, can be scheduled to convert files any time of the day or night, and can be set up to close program or shut down computer when done.

Alive MP3 WAV Converter 1.6.3.8 costs $29.95 for a single-user license. A fully functional trial version of Alive MP3 WAV Converter is available at website:www.alivemedia.net.
