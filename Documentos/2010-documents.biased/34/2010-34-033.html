Tuesday, December 8, 2009

Airplane blown off runway

BIRMINGHAM, AL (WBRC) - A small airplane was blown off the runway at the Birmingham airport Tuesday night.

Firefighters tell FOX6 News the small airplane was blown off the runway shortly after 8 p.m. Tuesday. One passenger was on board and was not hurt.

Very gusty winds were present at the time of the incident as a strong storm system moved through the area.

Birmingham Police and Fire officials were on the scene investigating.
