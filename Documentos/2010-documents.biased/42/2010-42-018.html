spike video game award: 2009

The Spike Video Game Awards (VGA) is an award show formed by the Spike television channel that recognizes the best computer and video games of the year. Started in 2003, the Spike TV Video Game Awards garnered much attention, since video game award shows were uncommon before its introduction.
