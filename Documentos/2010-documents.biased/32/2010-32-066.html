AMD Platforms 

A solid foundation to build on

We're the only company in the world to design both high-performance processors and world-class premium graphics cards. Because of this, AMD offers unparalleled platform features giving you flexibility, scalability and balance to help you get what you want out of your PC.

Our technologies are designed and built to work together at the system level for super-fast throughput, enhanced power efficiency, and a stunning visual experience.

We also offer special tools like AMD OverDrive™ that give you the ultimate control to monitor and customize your system's performance capabilities1.
 

AMD "Dragon" Platform Technology
The AMD platform technology codenamed "Dragon" is a synthesis of raw computing and graphics power. "Dragon" platform technology brings together the AMD Phenom™ II X4 Black Edition processor, the AMD 790 chipsets, GPUs like the ATI Radeon™ HD 4870 graphics card, and unique AMD software additions.

Building on "Dragon" platform technology helps provide you a system with legendary power. It's a PC platform designed for incredible control and an amazing visual experience. Gamers can dominate opponents in life-like environments with a new level of responsiveness and intensity.

"Maui"
Simplify your entertainment. "Maui" is the codename for AMD's Home Theater PC (HTPC) platform, bringing a full spectrum of digital entertainment into your living room. From downloading from Netflix and Hulu2, to playing the latest Blu-ray titles3, to watching cable TV4, to streaming online content to your TV2, and much more.

Building on the "Maui" HTPC platform starts with a high-performance, low-power CPU like one of our true multi-core AMD Phenom II processors. The "Maui" platform helps deliver brilliant HD video3 thanks in part to the AMD RS780M chipset and a full, rich Surround-Sound experience thanks to D2Audio Technology. Including a TV tuner card such as the ATI TV Wonder™ 650 PCIe® combo card opens up new worlds of digital television channels, delivered to your HDTV via the PC2.
