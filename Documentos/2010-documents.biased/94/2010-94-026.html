Apple Head

 

 

Apparently this is what you do with the sticker from your apple.  You stick it on the dogs head. 

Who knew.

And that was the 17 year old.

I shoveled the sidewalk and driveway but the snow is blowing enough that it pretty much covered everything I did. 

Sweet.

Some day I am going to leave the great white north for warmer climates.
