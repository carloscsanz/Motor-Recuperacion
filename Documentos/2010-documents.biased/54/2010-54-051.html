>> Re: Battlefield 2142 Sound problems with X-fi.
Odd, I have my X-Fi to game mode, and have the sounds in 2142 as high as they'll go, with no problems whatsoever. Have you downloaded the latest drivers? They just released a new set of drivers not even a few weeks ago.

Also, are you in Vista? Creative has had a lot of problems with Vista. Try this: right click "My Computer" and click properties. On the left, click Device Manager, and expand the Sound, Video, and Game Controllers tree. Right click your soundcard (Creative XB X-Fi) and disable it. Then, without rebooting, right click it again, and enable it.

I had some problems with my X-Fi early on in this build, and doing that would fix the problem, though sometimes it would come back when I rebooted. Hope that helps!
