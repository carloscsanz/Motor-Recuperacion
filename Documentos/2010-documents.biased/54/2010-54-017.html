The limited-edition Ableton nerdbag is about to sell out!

This edition is an exclusive design series from the German designer airbag craftworks, created with the Ableton style and sensibility in mind and produced only in limited numbers.

nerdbags are high-end notebook bags, known for their quality and functionality, and protected with a durable, weatherproof finish—designed by music lovers for music lovers, and made by hand in Germany. Each bag is unique and given its own personal ID number.

Founded in 1995, airbag craftworks has become synonymous with music, fashion and innovative style. In its quest to create the ideal laptop bag, airbag hit upon the design concept for the "nerdbag," which is a perfect fit for the Apple PowerBook series of notebooks, providing just the right mix of functionality, protection and charm.

All airbag craftworks products have three things in common. First, they are made from exceptional materials, such as fabric from air mattresses and rubber dinghies. Second, they exhibit a true love of craftwork — everything is designed and sewn at the airbag workshop in Germany. Third, airbag craftworks always aims to achieve the best functionality, the highest quality and the most exacting design standards possible, assuring the special quality of its products.

Each bag comes with a two-year warranty and is available exclusively via the Ableton webshop. Nerdbags come in a variety of sizes, from 15'', which is ready to ship, to custom 12'', 14'', 16'' and 17'' sizes, which are available upon request. Please allow an additional 14 days of production time for custom sizes.
