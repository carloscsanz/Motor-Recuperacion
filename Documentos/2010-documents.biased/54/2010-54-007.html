Description

Dell's no stranger to the idea of small form factor computers. They've offered slim desktops for years, and last year they introduced the interesting bamboo-covered Studio Hybrid desktop. The Inspiron Zino HD isn't quite as out there as the Studio Hybrid with its rounded square shape and brightly colored lid, but it definitely has its advantages.
