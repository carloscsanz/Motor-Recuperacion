05/03/2010 Review:
Nice Machine
review by: Trevs707 rating:
 operating system: Windows 7

Bought this on offer and used it for a week now.



Pros:

1. Buying from Ebuyer as it was delivered next day (although I was charged for delivery which has been subsequently refunded).

2. How quiet the machine runs.

3. The screen is very clear.

4. The weight is much less that the equivalent sized Advent laptop I am replacing.

5. General performance of the machine (i.e. word processing, spreadsheets and browsing). I haven’t tried any video editing/rendering on it yet.

6. The finish is good with a high gloss black lid and grey interior which feels well built.



Cons;

1. The touchpad is not central; it is offset to the left. It has taken me a bit of time to get use to. If Acer were going to offset the touchpad they probably would have been better doing it to the right as more people are right handed.

2. Not really a con as it is probably the same for any laptop you buy now but worth being aware of. Whilst the general set-up of the machine is simple and fast, you really need to create a set of recovery disks on DVD (3 for recovery +1 for drivers and apps) before you use it in anger. This takes about 1-hour+ as it needs to compile the image, burn and verify each disk to complete. It would have been better if Acer had either supplied the recovery disks with the laptop, or had the ISO image on the disk ready for you to burn, as it would have been much faster to complete.
