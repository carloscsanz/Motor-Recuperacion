MSI Working on �Powerless� CPU Cooler
  • 1
  • 2
  • 3
  • 4
  • 5
(2 votes)
Press Release
Written by Dimitar Dinchev a.k.a. Veseliq   
Sunday, 02 March 2008
 

MSI has taken a step to try and reduce computer power consumption slightly by building a processor fan that is powered using only heat given off by the CPU. As the processor heats up, it causes air inside a piston to expand. That forces out the piston rod, which in turn causes the fan to spin and blow air over the heatsink, as shown in the first image below. This then helps to cool the air inside the piston, causing it to contract again, pulling the piston rod back in and cooling the CPU without needing any extra electricity. The technology is by no means a modern idea � it uses a mechanism invented in 1816 by Scottish engineer Robert Stirling - but it's never before been used on a large scale for computing purposes. MSI�s fan is capable of converting 70% of the heat energy given off by the CPU into kinetic energy, and the company plans to demo the cooler at next week�s CeBIT show in Hanover. However, there is no news of when this fan will become available for purchase, or what prices will be like.

The MSI P7N ZILENT motherboard based on the NVIDIA nForce 750i chipset is now available. This limited edition motherboard is bundled with the award winning ZALMAN NP9500 Cooler. MSI offers with the MSI P7N ZILENT great value for money. The motherboard is equipped with the MSI Circu-Pipe cooler, combined with the ZALMAN cooler this provides extreme overclocking possibilities! The MSI P7N ZILENT supports FSB 1333MHz, 3 way NVIDIA SLI, PCI-Express generation 2.0, eSATA and is equipped with a TPM pin header. Further, the MSI P7N ZILENT is equipped with the special MSI GreenPower PWM design (PWM: Pulse Width Modulation). This is a dual channel 4-fase PWM. Each channel is built up by 2 shielded chokes (to prevent power leakage) and a MOSFET base which provides the highest power efficiency. This new technology leads the motherboard�s power design in fewer phases to provide better power supply.

  • Based on Nvidia nForce 750i SLI Chipset
  • Bundled with ZALMAN NP9500 cooler
  • Supports Intel 45 nm Penryn / Quad core / Core 2 Duo processors
  • Supports Dual Ch. DDR2-1066/800/667
  • 3 PCI Express x16 slots, nVidia SLI
  • PCI-Express generation 2.0
  • TPM Pinheader
  • Award winning MSI Circu-Pipe
  • Supports eSATA
  • GBLan and FireWire
