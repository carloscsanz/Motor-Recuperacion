    Works pretty well with Vista

    Reviewed By: Matt on 7/17/2009
    Tech Level: somewhat high - Ownership: 1 month to 1 year
    This user purchased this item from Newegg
    Pros: Pretty easy to setup and get working with a Dell Dimension laptop running Vista. All other USB devices are plugged into the dock and working fine.
    Cons: In terms of mirroring the video between the laptop and LCD connected to this dock, it's a little sketchy. Most of the time we have to go into the Kensington Dock tray menu system, select Mirror and hope it works. It would be nice if the driver would remember mirroring the video as the default behavior.
    Other Thoughts: Just fix the default video behavior and this product would be much more user friendly.

    Has worked well for me

    Reviewed By: on 1/30/2009
    Tech Level: high - Ownership: more than 1 year
    This user purchased this item from Newegg
    Pros: Works with different types of notebooks. Only one cable to connect/disconnect. Network, audio and any USB devices I have plugged in to the dock have worked without any problems at all. The network speed is faster than any of the built in wireless G interfaces.
    Cons: Video resolution for the display connected to the expansion dock is tied to the video resolution for the notebook.
    Other Thoughts: I'm not aware of a good solution that will work for different types of notebooks using a single cable to connect/disconnect that works better. I did a lot of shopping around in 2006 and did not find anything even close to this. At the time of purchase (I bought two of these on two different dates in 2007) Newegg had the best price I could find.
