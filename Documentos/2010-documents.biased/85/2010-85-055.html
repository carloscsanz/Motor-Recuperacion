Hi folks,

I wonder if this has been asked before, but what is the best benchmarking tool out there for the graphics card? I know there are loads of benchmarks testing CPUs and multithreading, but this market is still kinda small when it comes to 3D and graphics cards..

I mean what benchmark tools are there except for 3dmark? And it is known that 3dmark is not such a good benchmark tool because your CPU can influence it a lot and is therefore not a good way of comparing graphics cards.

Sounds good? Does it exist? not that I know. So what would still be the best way for graphics card benching???
