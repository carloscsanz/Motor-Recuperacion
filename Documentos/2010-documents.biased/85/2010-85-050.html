User group leaders volunteer incredible amounts of time and effort for their groups. Fortunately, the benefits are clear to leaders, members, and the community. All groups benefit if leaders help each other and have access to tools and resources to run their groups. This help is here.



Apple User Group Bulletin. Leaders of registered groups can subscribe to receive exclusive event information and product discounts.

Apple User Group Discussion List. A place for Apple user group leaders to exchange ideas and discuss topics related to Apple user groups.

User Group Advisory Board. The Advisory Board helps represent your needs to Apple. Contact the UGAB with any ideas, suggestions, or concerns.

Apple User Group Regional Liaisons. User group leaders who run successful user groups in their communities. As volunteers, they have made themselves available to help groups in their areas of responsibility thrive.

Campus Group Advisory Board. Campus user group leaders who can share their knowledge, experiences and passion with other students across the world.
