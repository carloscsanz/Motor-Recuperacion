    Windows Media Player 11 for XP release details
    < Previous | Next >
    Posted by badrix 1314 Day ago, There are 45 comments, 69463 views
    WMP 11 has been on the news for XP, is a reluctance to disclose the names of the Microsoft employees issued, Microsoft in May 17 had issued a public version of RTM forthcoming release later this year, the fastest on the 14th next month.
    Following is the updated list and RTM WMP before the improvements :

    Features in WMP 11 for XP (June 06) :
    * New UI
    * Support for better synchronization for portable devices
    * Album "stacks" of album art
    * Indexed libraries with search-as-you-type 3-6
    * User controlled downgrade of quality for DRM content to allow for smaller files
    * DRM content will contain metadata that shows what the user is allowed to do with the file
    * Includes Microsoft and MTV 's Urge Shakespeare store

    Features in WMP 11 for Vista (King 07) :
    * Everything that is in the WMP 11 for XP
    * Playing content (including DRM) on your PC from another PC or device
    * Viewing content from the Vista Media Available on PCs or other digital devices, such as Tivo
    * Playback of High Value video content
    * shelf integration with Windows Media Player
    * Content Indexer change notification to sync My Music and WMP library
    * Fullscreen DVD playback enhancements
    * DRM Transcode
    * High quality video streaming over home networks
    * Media foundation for playback
