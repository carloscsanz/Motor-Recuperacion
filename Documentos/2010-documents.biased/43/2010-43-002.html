Google Caffeine is presently live on one Google data center

Dec 01
2009
Leave a
Comment

As we all know a few days back Google unveiled their next generation advanced search engine known as Google Caffeine is now live on one of their data center. As confirmed Matt Cutts it may not be included to more Google data centers until after the holidays.

As mentioned by the Matt Cutts of Google confirmed that the IP address 209.85.225.103 is getting more hits about 50% of the time for the data center on which Caffeine index is running currently. Matt Cutts clarify that the latest update webmasters were observing were also this data center or individual sites remain penalize  in some sort.

Previously mentioned, Google Caffeine is now live on a single data center which is presently available about 50% of the time on IP 209.85.225.103. Matt did not reveal if we can get the results with the new update 100% of the time from a particular IP.

Google Features 10 Millions servers

Oct 25
2009
Leave a
Comment

It will be funny to introduce you to Google, one of the strongest brand in the world and king of the web world. We all appreciate the service provided by Google, but ever wondered the reason behind this, well the reason is the 10 million server of Google situated in Google’s many Data Centers. Google’s Data Center are situated  in almost 18 locations in United States of America and 19 Global locations like London, Paris, Milan, Sao Paolo, Zurich and many more. Google is preparing for more servers in the future as Google’s business is growing on an exponential rate. Google’s speaker  Jeff Dean revealed about some of Google’s upcoming Data Centers at ACM workshop. Spanner a new technical storage system was also intruduced to the world. Spanner will work as a automated management for Google  Data Center. working range for Spanner is huge, working for 1 million to 10 million servers.
