The National Summit Unveils America's
To-Do List

The organizers of The National Summit met with Secretary of Commerce Gary Locke in Washington on December 14 to discuss and unveil Americas To-Do List. The ten strategic initiatives on the list are a summary of the consensus recommendations made at The National Summit last June. The Book of Proceedings, a more detailed and comprehensive account of the 45 hours of discussions that took place at The National Summit, will be available soon.
