Screw Notepad. Go back into Word.
- Select all text with Ctrl+A
- Reformat the document in Courier New 10 pt.
- Save your document as plain text.
- Re-Open your text document.
- Select all text with Ctrl+A.
- On the formatting toolbar, justify it with the button on the right, which looks like five closely spaced, equal length, parallel lines. If you are unsure of how to do this, open Help and look up "justify text."
Now that you have justified it, save it again.

Your document should now be in plain text with both margins justified. Note that MLA and APA style both eschew justifying the right margin, so look for those bombs in your assignment specs.
