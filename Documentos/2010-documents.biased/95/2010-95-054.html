The dawn of a new Ice Age has thrown the world into a panic - what soil not already covered by ice can only feed a fraction of the Earth's population. The math is simple and brutal... some will live, most will die. In Battlefield 2142, you choose to fight for one of two military superpowers, the European Union or the newly-formed Pan Asian Coalition, in a brutal war for the only cause that matters - survival.

Get armed with a devastating arsenal of hi-tech weaponry, including assault rifles, cloaking devices and sentry guns, and take control of the most lethal vehicles known to man.

# A Whole New Battlefield - The award-winning franchise blasts into the distant future. With Earth's icecaps threatening to cover the globe, civilizations must push toward the equator in an all-out war for every resource on every inch of land.
# Titans Rule the Skies - These massive airborne carriers/warships serve as home bases. In the first-ever new gameplay mode in the Battlefield universe, teams must work together to destroy the enemy Titan. To defeat a Titan, you must first take down its shields, board the craft and fight to its inner core before detonating it from the inside. This genre leading gameplay innovation changes the way you play, encouraging both teamplay and strategy in explosive large-scale confrontations.
# A Powerful Futuristic Arsenal - Fight the war of the future with devastating weapons like the NK-1000 Type 2 sniper rifle, deadly gadgets like the SD-8 Accipiter drone, and next-generation vehicles such as the mighty L-5 Riesig Battle Walkers. Use your wits and an arsenal of new hi-tech countermeasures such as EMP grenades and smart mines to level the playing field.
# Customize Your Soldier - Build up your soldier's attributes as you see fit with more than 1500 combinations in the new skill tree system - fight as a medic sniper or a recon engineer.
# Unlock the Future - With in-game success, you increase your rank and unlock awards, delivering the most comprehensive and flexible persistence in the Battlefield universe to date. Battlefield 2142 boasts five times the number of upgrades in Battlefield 2, with more than 40 ranks and unlocks including special squad and commander upgrades.
# The World has Changed! - Fight for survival on the Battlefield of the 22nd century in new locations from Minsk to North Africa as the ice presses down from the North.
# Epic Online Gameplay - Up to 64 players on the front lines as part of a formal squad, or behind the scenes in Commander Mode to direct the strategic assaults of their team-mates.
