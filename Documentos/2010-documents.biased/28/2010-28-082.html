Spike 2009 Video Game Awards Results

PS3, Wii, Xbox360 | Brandon Hofer | December 12, 2009 at 10:34 pm

The Spike TV 2009 Video Game Awards just ended and here are the nominees as well as the winners.

Game of the Year

     Assassin’s Creed II

     Batman Arkham Asylum

     Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2

     Left 4 Dead 2

     Uncharted 2: Among Thieves - WINNER

Studio of the Year

     Naughty Dog (Uncharted 2: Among Thieves)

     Infinity Ward (Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2)

     Rocksteady Studios (Batman Arkham Asylum) - WINNER

     Valve (Left 4 Dead 2)

Best Independent Game Fueled By Dew

     ‘Splosion Man

     Flower - WINNER

     Osmos

     Trials HD

Best XBox360 Game

     Left 4 Dead 2 - WINNER

Best PS3 Game

     Uncharted 2: Among Thieves - WINNER

Best Wii Game

     New Super Mario Bros Wii - WINNER

Best PC Game

     Dragon Age Origins - WINNER

Best Handheld Game

     Grand Theft Auto: Chinatown Wars - WINNER

Best Shooter

     Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2 - WINNER

     Killzone 2

     Left 4 Dead 2

     Halo 3: ODST

Best Fighting Game

     Street Fighter IV - WINNER

Best Action Adventure Game

     Assassin’s Creed II - WINNER

     Batman Arkham Asylum

     Brutal Legend

     Uncharted 2: Among Thieves

Best Role Playing Game

     Borderlands

     Demon’s Souls

     Mario & Luigi: Bowser’s Inside Story

     Dragon Age Origins - WINNER

Best Multi-Player Game

     Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2 - WINNER

Best Individual Sports Game

     Fight Night Round 4

     Tiger Woods PGA Tour 10

     Wii Sports Resort

     UFC 2009 Undisputed - WINNER

Best Team Sports Game

     Madden NFL 10

     NBA 2K10

     NHL 10 - WINNER

     FIFA Soccer 10

Best Driving Game

     Forza Motorsport 3 - WINNER

Best Music Game

     DJ Hero

     Guitar Hero 5

     Lego Rock Band

     The Beatles: Rock Band - WINNER

Best Soundtrack

     DJ Hero - WINNER

Best Original Score

     Halo 3: ODST - WINNER

Best Graphics

     Uncharted 2: Among Thieves - WINNER

Best Game Based On A Movie/TV Show

     South Park: Let’s Go Tower Defense Play - WINNER

Best Performance By A Human Female

     Megan Fox (Transformers: Revenge of the Fallen) - WINNER

Best Performance By A Human Male

     Hugh Jackman (X-Men Origins Wolverine Uncaged Edition) - WINNER

Best Cast

     X-Men Origins Wolverine Uncaged Edition - WINNER

Best Voice

     Arleen Sorkin (Harley Quinn in Arkham Asylum)

     Claudia Black (Chloe Frazier in Uncharted 2: Among Thieves)

     Jack Black (Eddie Riggs in Brutal Legend) - WINNER

     Nolan North (Nathan Drake in Uncharted 2: Among Thieves)

     Mark Hamill (Joker in Arkham Asylum)

Best Downloadable Game

     Shadow Complex - WINNER

Best Downloadable Content

     Grand Theft Auto: The Ballad of Gay Tony - WINNER

Most Anticipated Game

     God of War III - WINNER
