How I got my blog indexed by Google in 1 day!

Jul 31, 2007

When I first got into blogging, I didn’t have much idea about how can I get my blog (ReviewSaurus – Free software reviews)indexed by Google fast and it took quite some time for me to get my blog indexed by Google. Now, that I have a fair amount of idea about search engine optimization (I’m not a pro in SEO), I managed to get my this blog (TipsoSaurus – Tips and tricks for computers) crawl by GoogleBot in 1 day only. It may not be a very difficult task for many pro’s but I hope that new people will find this tips helpful. Here’s what I did to get this blog indexed fast :

1. Google Webmaster Tools : Remember, Google is your friend and so, don’t forget to tell it that you’ve started a new blog and that it should visit your blog. For that, make use of Google Webmaster tools and submit your website or blog address.


(The blog was launched on 29th and check when GoogleBot Crawled this blog. The very same day)

2. Using Sitemap : Don’t forget to generate the sitemap of the blog and submit it to the Google Webmaster Tools. Also, keep in mind that you link to that sitemap from your webpages, that way Google will have no chance of missing it.

3. Comment on blogs with Do Follow or Top Commentator : If you know any blog which has Do Follow applied on comments then go ahead and leave some meaningful comments on those blogs. If you know a blog which has Top Commentator plugin installed then you can leave some meaningful comment on those blogs too and get into the list of Top commentator as you’ll get a link back, if you crack in that list.

I hope these simple tips will help you in getting into the Google’s Index faster than ever before. In case you have some tips to share, please share them with us in the comments section.
