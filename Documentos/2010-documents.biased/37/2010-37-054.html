O2 Hires Former Blyk Chief Executive as Managing Director

Written by Doug Coleman / February 9, 2009 9:02 AM / 0 Comments
« Prior Post Next Post »

UK telecommunications giant O2 announced today that it has hired Shaun Gregory as its new Managing Director of its media business, a division of Telefónica O2 UK. He will begin March 1st.

Prior to joining O2, Gregory was Chief executive at UK mobile network Blyk. Before that he was New Media Director at Telegraph Media Group.
