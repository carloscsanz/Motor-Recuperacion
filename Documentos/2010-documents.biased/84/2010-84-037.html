CHICAGO -- Steve Ballmer, Chief Executive Officer of Microsoft Corporation, will address the members and guests of The Executives' Club of Chicago at its June 18, 2009 Global Leaders luncheon program. Ballmer will speak on "The Role of Innovation in Changing Economic Times."

Ballmer joined Microsoft in 1980 and was the first business manager hired by Bill Gates. Since then, Ballmer's leadership and passion have become hallmarks of his tenure at the company. Ballmer and the company's business and technical leaders are focused on continuing Microsoft's innovation and leadership across the company's core business.
