Laptop Bag

Not a single person who was present at the unveiling of the first ever computer would have imagined that a computer could be carried around. But, with the advent of laptops things took an altogether different turn. These computers became a constant companion for many of the corporate and freelancer types, but as they were expensive and delicate, a proper protective case became necessary and the laptop bags came into existence. Today, the laptop bag, also known as the laptop computer case and laptop carrying case, has become one of the largest selling products for a number of bag manufacturers. The note book case, referred to as the laptop bag in certain parts of the world, is often provided by the notebook companies themselves.

Depending on the size of the laptop, the laptop computer case comes in varying sizes. The sizes range from 15 inches to 20 inches in breadth. The laptop bag is made of various materials like rexine, leather, hard pvc, vinyl and so on. Some laptop bags are soft and have a reinforced metal frame. This makes the bag gentle on the notebook surface but at the same time offers protection from impact and pressure related damages.

Certain models of the laptop bags have a waterproof inner lining to prevent the laptop from getting wet. To add to the protective feature, the laptop bags also come with cushion padding inside the case. Some laptop bags also sport a number lock, which prevents unauthorized access. To make carrying the laptop bag around easier, a shoulder strap is attached to the bag. A generous amount of padding within the shoulder straps makes for better load distribution and proves easier on the shoulder. The laptop bags also have a number of pockets and compartments in them. This feature makes it easier and comfortable to carry accessories like the laptop adapter, the charger, cables, USB drives, Bluetooth devices, CDs, DVDs etc. along with the laptop.

Laptop bags, apart from being merely a storage and protection kit for the notebooks, have today become a symbol of style and status. As a result, laptop bags are designed for catering to people from every walk of life. The business class people who are keen in making a style statement in the corporate world generally use the leather laptop bags. For the people who are constantly on the move there are travel cases, which have a separate compartment for the laptops. This makes the travel easier as the need to carry a separate bag for laptop is eliminated. There are sport bags that can be worn on the back. This type of a laptop bag comes handy when the journey also includes walking. For the fashion conscious lot, there are the designer laptop bags to carry around their laptops, in a manner that reflects their high tastes in style. Buying a laptop carrying case from an online marketing site gives you a choice of choosing anything ranging in between a cheap laptop bag and an extravagant, expensive laptop bag.

Laptop stands are used to position the laptops at comfortable heights and angulations, especially when being used for a long duration. The laptop stands often come with an additional keyboard, multiple USB ports and laptop charger.
