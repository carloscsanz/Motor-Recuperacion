    Chances are Halo 3 won't be coming out for the PC for another 6 months or so, since that's typically how long it takes before they release a PC port after they release the console version of games. As for if the game will run on XP or Vista there has been no announcements of that, so nobody knows as of right now.


    EDIT: Yes, I realize Halo and Halo 2 took a long dang time to come out with a PC version, but I'm looking mostly at the overall release dates for PC ports of 360 games these days. Yes, Gears of Wars took awhile to come out for the PC, but games such as Bioshock, Tomb Raider Anniversay, Lost Planet: Extreme Conditions, and Ratatouille all came out relative quickly after their 360 versions released. Games that are coming out soon like Stranglehold and Crysis are getting their PC ports almost simultaneously to their console version releases, so PC ports for 360 games seem to be getting faster as time goes along.
