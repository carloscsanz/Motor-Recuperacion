(50) - About Chief Executive


Chief Executive magazine, founded in 1977, is the source of information and intelligence that supports the chief executive officer. It is the only magazine written strictly for CEOs and their peers and is the leading source of intelligence for and about CEOs. Chief Executive provides ideas, strategies and tactics for top executive leaders who are seeking to build more effective organizations. It conveys the full range of the CEO experience: the corporate challenges as well as the personal pressures and satisfactions that come with the most demanding job in business. The magazine serves its CEO audience via a variety of media, including print, in-person and online, fostering opportunities for CEOs to share their experiences and expertise within a community of peers. Chief Executive events bring CEOs together through its annual CEO2CEO Leadership Summit, open to C-suite executives, its annual CEO of the Year celebration, and its CEO Roundtables.

Chief Executive's 'CEO Confidence Index' offers a unique perspective on the economy and future business plans. The CEO Confidence Index measures the confidence that CEOs have in the economy as well as their future business plans, and is unique both in its focus and survey base. The survey also includes a provocative ‘bonus question’ created by Chief Executive's editorial team. The CEO Confidence Index results are posted on Chief Executive website and covered within the magazine. Other business media outlets look to the CEO Confidence Index as one of the indicators of the economic trends. Chief Executive is published eight times a year and delivered to a controlled circulation of 42,025 CEOs, Presidents, Chairmen, Vice-Chairmen and other top management executives. Total readership exceeds 228,000.
