Happy Thanksgiving

Posted on November 22nd, 2007 by Mad Bull

Happy Thanksgiving to all of you who celebrate that. That includes my Caymanian bredrins and sistren, incidentally, who go around the place wishing all and sundry “Happy Thanksgiving”, even though its a U.S. holiday and not a Caymanian one. I found that rather interesting. I am not going to knock it too much still, because I have been invited to a Thanksgiving dinner and I like turkey still, so…

People, I haven’t forgotten you! Its just that I-an-I have been quite busy, rasta! I am talking about the Gym! Plus work! Plus setting up PCs for a couple of other people and preparing to rebuild my own PC. (i.e. Getting codes for the O/S I plan to use! Locating all the drivers! Trying to figure out the best way to handle the upgrade on a dual boot system I have, etc.) Plus preparing to load a new file unto my free-to-air satellite receiver (locating the right file, doing the reading, making sure you have the right loader, etc…). And then more of the same! Busy!

I am going to try to write a post or two between now and Monday though. Look out for a little sup’m sup’m. Anyway, since I have not written anything for y’all for a bit and since I am not posting a blockbuster post for you tonight, I have decided to give you a little game to play instead. The game is contained in an Excel spreadsheet, and you have to download it to play it. Now all I can offer you in the way of assurances that nothing will happen to your PC if you download and run this spreadsheet is that I have had it and been playing it for more than a week now and my PC is great! Also, I know a fair number of people who have also downloaded the game and been playing it, and they’re good too! If something should happen to your PC because you downloaded or played this game, me nuh response! If you have issues with that, do not download the game! Ok… now that the disclaimer is out of the way, on to the game!

The game involves trying to “throw” a paper plane and getting it to go as far as possible. I sent it to Seven by email and she was trying to dust me out, but so far, she hasn’t! I challenge you all to try to dust me out! So far, I have gotten it to go 53+ metres. Think you can do better? Just click on the picture above showing the distance I threw the plane to download the game, seen? Its better you save the spreadsheet on your hard drive and then execute it, rather than just running it straight. Have fun, and I bet you can’t beat my score!

Anyway, TTYL still! Going to go nyam some turkey!
