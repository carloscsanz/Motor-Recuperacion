Embedded and Kiosk Systems

 

EMBEDDED SYSTEMS

Embedded systems are computer structures, usually hidden in devices and equipment, such as mobile phones, various machines and devices, cars, aeroplanes, processes management systems and large infrastructure systems.

There has been a largely growing interest in such systems in the recent years. Today, embedded systems are playing an important role in the sector of information technology.

  

Services of designing such systems include: 

  • Embedded systems planning
  • The making of custom embedded operating
    systems
  • The making of software for the need of embedded systems

KIOSK SYSTEMS

Interactive kiosks are systems with a computer terminal which gives access to information with the help of electronics. Kiosks can usually be found in places where there is a large number of people passing by, for instance in public transport, hotels, city centres etc.

The integration of technologies in the kiosks as such ensures a great usefulness. Such kiosk systems enable access to bank accounts, bank transactions and cash withdrawals. They are also used to get access to on-line information as well as selling a wide range of products. Custom optional components are coin and bill validators, card readers and thermal printers. They enable the kiosk systems to act in a certain way, according to our client's wishes.


 

Types of kiosk systems:

  • Bank kiosk systems
  • Photo kiosk systems – used for ordering photos from several photo storage media
  • Internet kiosks – access to internet
  • Ticket kiosks – designed for any kind of ticket sale
  • Vending machines – designed for several kinds of products – beverages, food and others
  • Information kiosk systems – usually these can be found in shopping centres, at museums, fairs...
