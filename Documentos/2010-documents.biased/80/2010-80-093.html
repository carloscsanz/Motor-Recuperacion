Today was the “Spike’s Video Game Awards” filled with new trailers, awards, music performances, and cheesy jokes. Here is the overall result of the show.  No big surprises with the winners, games you would think would be the winner probably were the winner, but I thought the best part of the entire show was the start with the new Batman trailer. All this week Videos of the show/trailers I will be posting so stay tuned
