 Reader's Rigs - Oc Master
 By: Dave Crewe | Edited by  | Published: November 8, 2005  

   

Username?

Oc Master

Age/Occupation/Location?

15 years/ don�t have a real job but I do yard work and friends and families houses/ I live in Lynnwood Washington about 20 miles from Seattle

How long have you been a member of Guru3D forums? 

About a year. I came across the site one day when looking for advice about how much RAM was needed for the latest games.

What did you first come here for?

I first came here regarding how much RAM I should get and should I replace my one 512 stick of pc-2700 with two 512 sticks pc-3200. The answer was obviously switch it out and get PC-3200

What do you mainly use the system for?

I mainly use my system for gaming whenever possible. Another task I use my rig for is school work which really sucks.

How much did you spend on the rig?

I spent about 1200 dollars on my rig. I had to buy it in increments because of the fact that I only do yard work for money, and my parents don�t believe in allowance so I was out of luck there.

How often do you update/buy a new rig?

I probably update every other month depending on what I have in cash leftover. I build a completely new rig about every 5 years or so.

What components are used?

Mobo: Shuttle AN35N-Ultra
CPU: AMD ATHLON 2700+ overclocked to 2.28 GHZ @ 11*207
RAM: A pair of Kingmax PC-3200 Value Ram And a stick of 512 generic
GPU: PNY 6600GT with a Zalman VF700-CU Overclocked to 570 core and 1115 memory
Sound Card: Sound Blaster LIVE! 24-bit edition
Hard Drive(s): One Maxtor 80 gig ATA 133 and one 80 gig Seagate ATA 133

Why ATI or NVIDIA, why Intel/AMD? 

I chose NVIDIA because the 6600 GT was one of the best buys for the price and I was on a very tight budget and needed something that could perform nicely but was cheap. I chose AMD because the Intel chips were too much money and AMD�s performance was much better than that of Intel's.

Does the system work as expected?

Yeah works great for what I play and do. Games like battlefield 2 and Medal of Honour Pacific Assault play like a dream on this system.

What's the best part of your rig?

The best part of my rig is probably my graphics card because without I wouldn�t be able to play games on such a high resolution or with all the other effects on.

What sucks and should not have been bought?

Well I originally bought one 512 stick of pc-2700 and wasn�t successful in overclocking my system so I had to go and get some better RAM. Also the case I've got isn't the best looking. I looked at and first thought it looked cool but now I don�t like the look of it.

Modified cases/video cards/other? Did you do it yourself? Walk us through it.

Well the first modification I did was to get some black light and some UV reactive sleeves for my wires. Then I cut out the back fan holes to increase airflow and decrease noise. Then I recently got a zalman VF700-cu cooler for my graphics card that dropped my temp from a sizzling 85 degrees Celsius load to a nice and cool 48 degrees Celsius load. 

3DMark scores?

3dmark03 score is 8712
3dmark05 score is 3870
Aquamark score is 52,300

Future upgrades? 

Maybe a better graphics card if they still sell AGP cards when I go and upgrade.
