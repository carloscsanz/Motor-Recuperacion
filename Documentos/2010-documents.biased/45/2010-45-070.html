Spike TV 2009 Video Game Award Winners

Jason Wong • Dec 17, 2009  • Comments (0)

Another year, another award show -- but wait! Here's a show we gamers might actually wanna watch. Yes, it's the Spike TV 2009 Video Game Awards and we've got your winners right here.

The show was worth watching purely for the joy of ogling Olivia Wilde's hotness, but we digress.

Hand me the envelop, please:


Best Cast and Best Performance by a Human Male - Hugh Jackman (X-Men Origins: Wolverine)
Best Shooter and Multi-Player Game - Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2
Studio of the Year - Rocksteady Studios
Best Music Game - The Beatles: Rock Band
Best Handheld Game - Grand Theft Auto: Chinatown Wars
Best Driving Game - Forza Motorsport 3
Best Action Adventure Game - Assassin's Creed II
Best XBox 360 Game - Left 4 Dead 2
Best Wii Game - New Super Mario Bros. Wii
Best PS3 Game - Uncharted 2: Among Thieves
Best PC Game - Dragon Age: Origins
Best Soundtrack - DJ Hero
Best Original Score - Halo 3: ODST
Best Game Based on a Movie or TV Show - South Park Let's Go Tower Defense Play!
Best Performance by a Human Female - Megan Fox (Transformers: Revenge of the Fallen)
Best Voice - Jack Black as Eddie Riggs (Brutal Legend)
Best Downloadable Game - Shadow Complex
Best Comedy Game - Eat Lead: The Return of Matt Hazard
Most Anticipated Game - God of War III

Best Video Game of the Year - Uncharted 2: Among Thieves
