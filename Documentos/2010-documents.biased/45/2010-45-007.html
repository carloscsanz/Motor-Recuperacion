Spike TV Video Game Award Winners Announced

Nov 19, 2005 at 12:25 PM - - 0 Comments
Results from the Spike TV Video Game Awards filmed last night; winners highlighted in bold:

  • Call of Duty 2 (Activision, Inc.)
