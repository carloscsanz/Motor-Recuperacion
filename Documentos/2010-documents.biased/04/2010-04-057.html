ESTATE TOY AUCTION

Auction will have both live bidders and online bidders through Proxibid.com

SATURDAY, JANUARY 23RD AT 10 A.M.

INSPECTION BEGINS AT 8 A.M.

Auction is hosted online thru www.Proxibid.com/belchermcpherson for those who cannot attend the auction.

 The Edwin Waldron Collection of toys will be sold at public auction with no minimums and no reserves.  Mr. Waldrons collection consisted of early American toys from Buddy L, Cor Cor, Smith Miller, Keystone, Doepke, Wyandotte, Kingsbury, Steelcraft, Metalcraft, Tonka and more plus some contemporary toys and displays from the likes of Hallmark, Ertl and a large collection of Department 56 collectables.  Some of the items are in there original condition and others have been restored by Mr. Waldron himself.  His restoration work is second to none and in many cases, these pieces look as good as the day they were produced.

LOCATED:  The Robins Nest Banquet Center  - 16181 10 Mile Road, Battle Creek, Michigan.  DIRECTIONS:  From I-94 between Marshall and Battle Creek take the 11 Mile Road Exit (104) then South to 1st light (Michigan Ave.) then right approximately 1 mile to 10 mile road then right 300 to banquet facility entrance. 

There are 272 catalog lots that will be offered with live bidding and on-line bidding thru Proxibid.  Highlights of this collection include:  BUDDY L:  Scarce 1920s  airport hanger with one monoplane; Large trucks including lumber truck, street sprinkler, Ice Truck, Railway Express Truck, Moving Van, International Harvester Sales and Service truck; Sand and Gravel truck; Aerial Ladder truck; Auto Wrecker, Hyd. Dump Truck, and more!!!; outdoor railroad engine and tender plus the T-Reproductions Caboose, Side Ballast, Bottom Dump, flat car, box car, tank car, gondola and caboose; Gravel Loader & concrete mixer;  Flivver Roadster; (2) Flivver pickup trucks; KEYSTONE:  Koaster Truck; Hydraulic Lift dump truck; No. 78 Wrecker; U.S. Mail Truck; Ambulance; Police Patrol; Packard Water Tower Firetruck; Chemical pump engine; STURDITOYS:  Water Tower NO. 9 Firetruck; Pumper NO. 7; American Railway Express; Dairy Company Delivery van; U.S. Mail truck; SMITH MILLER:  GMC Dump; Pie Mack Truck; Mobil Tanker; Bank of America Truck; GMC Mobil Oil truck; Stake Truck; Wrecker; Ladder truck; DOEPKE:  Ladder Truck; Jaguar XK-120; MG Roaster; COR COR:  Step Van, Side Dump truck; Rear Dump Truck; Floor Train; RETRO:  Overland Tour bus and Greyhound Lines bus; PLUS MUCH MORE!!!!  Call for a free catalog or check out our web site for catalog and all photos. TERMS:  Cash/Check/Visa, M.C. & Discover.  A 10% buyers premium will be charged on all live auction purchases for this auction.  An additional 3% buyers premium will be charged for credit card purchases.  Statements day of sale take precedence over printed matter.  Food and restrooms on site. Remember to bring your packing boxes!!  NOTE:  The Hallmark and Department 56 collectables will be sold to live auction bidders only immediately following the catalog portion of this auction.
