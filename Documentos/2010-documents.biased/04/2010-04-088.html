Medicare Australia (Functions of Chief Executive Officer - Call Centre Services) Direction 2009

Legislative Instrument - F2009L04417

 

Brief Description: This Direction confers on the Chief Executive Officer of Medicare Australia the function of providing call centre services on behalf of a Commonwealth authority or a State authority.
Classification: Directions
Date Registered:12/03/2009
Date of Making:11/19/2009
Tabled HR:02/02/2010
Tabled Senate:02/02/2010
