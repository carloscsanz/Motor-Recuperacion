Sporting a stylish, sleek and modern design, our new cotton candy pink patent laptop bags are both colorful and functional. This new laptop bag will definitely turn heads when carried to your next meeting or social gathering.
 

The Black Candy laptop bag has two roomy, padded compartments... one for your laptop and one for everything else. It includes plenty of space for your folders, notebooks, cell phone, PDA, wallet, makeup, USB flash drives, power cord and a section of pockets to organize business cards, pens, etc.
 

The padded laptop compartment holds the removable, checkpoint-friendly laptop sleeve. With the recent passage of new security rules, this bag's design allows for easy airport security passage without having to remove the laptop from the sleeve. Simply remove the laptop sleeve from the bag, place in on the x-ray conveyor and breeze through the security line.
 

The second compartment has zippered pockets in three sizes, along with a large mesh pocket to hold folders or a power cord. This section also contains a pen holder and business card pocket, with a transparent front for easy identification.
 

The back of the bag has an outside zippered pocket for quick access to your keys, phone, wallet or travel documents. On the bottom of the bag you'll find four silver feet to protect the bag from wetness, dirt or scratches.
 

Stylish design... beautiful colors... an assortment of pockets... padded protection... you'll be proud to carry this bag and your laptop will never want to leave it!
 

*Will my laptop fit?: Fits all laptops with screen sizes up to 15.4" (with included sleeve) and some 17" (without sleeve). The laptop sleeve included with this bag fits laptops up to 15.4" and some 17" laptops will fit without the sleeve. Since the bag itself is padded, the bag can be used without a sleeve and still provide protection to your laptop. If you're unsure about the size of your laptop, please contact us and we'll look up the dimensions for you to let you know if your laptop will fit.

Acer, Apple (MacBook Air, 13.3" MacBook, 15.4" & 17" MacBook Pro, iBook G4, PowerBook G4), Compaq, Dell (Inspiron, Latitude, XPS), Dell Studio 15, Fujitsu, Gateway, HP, IBM / Lenovo ThinkPad's & 3000 series, Sony Vaio's, Toshiba and many others.
