Welcome!

Welcome to Harzing.com, Anne-Wil Harzing's website.

Anne-Wil is Professor in International Management at the University of Melbourne, Australia.

This website provides information, online papers and resources about her areas of research. It also presents resources to assist with academic publishing and the assessment of research and journal quality, as well as software to conduct citation analysis.
