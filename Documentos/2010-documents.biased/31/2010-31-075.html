JOBCAST

JOBCAST is NJN's first digitally televised and webcast program and is aimed at preparing individuals for employment in the public and private sectors. About eight-minutes in length, programs feature career profiles, success stories, job search tips, or opportunities within New Jersey's workplace.

Following is a selection of JOBCAST shows which may be of particular interest to anyone who has been incarcerated. After viewing these selections of JOBCAST segments, we invite you to view other JOBCAST shows. Click here for a complete list of archived shows.

Open Captioning of JOBCAST episodes is now available at all Workforce Learning Link sites to accommodate hearing-impaired learners.
