Company Overview 

Hamilton Sundstrand is a global supplier of technologically advanced aerospace and industrial products. We design and manufacture aerospace systems for over 90 percent of the world’s commercial, regional, corporate and military aircraft. Headquartered in Windsor Locks, Connecticut, we have approximately 18,300 people at more than 50 major facilities internationally, making us among the world’s largest suppliers. Hamilton Sundstrand Industrial consists of three separate companies: Milton Roy, Sullair and Sundyne. Our industrial products serve industries ranging from hydrocarbon, chemical and food processing to construction mining. Come be a part of our $5 billion company and join the leader in aerospace and industrial systems.

Hamilton Sundstrand is a proud member of the United Technologies Corporation (UTC) family of companies. UTC is a diversified FORTUNE 50 Company whose products include Carrier HVAC and refrigeration systems; Otis elevators, escalators and horizontal transportation systems; Pratt & Whitney aircraft engines, turbines and space propulsion systems; Sikorsky helicopters; UTC Fire & Security systems and services; UTC Power fuel cells; and our stand alone Research Center.

Job Description 

Hamilton Sundstrand is among the largest global suppliers of technologically advanced aerospace and industrial products. We design and manufacture aerospace systems for commercial, regional, corporate and military aircraft and are a major supplier for international space programs. Our industrial products serve industries ranging from hydrocarbon, chemical and food processing to construction and mining.


Design, implement, & support the ODACS software framework used for real-time Automatic Test Equipment applications using the C++ programming language on various UNIX operating systems ( LynxOS, Linux, etc.)
Provide direct support to our internal customers in the test labs.
Generate and maintain software documentation and change requests.
 


Job Requirements 

BSEE / BSCE, or a BSCS with emphasis on technical UNIX applications.

4 to 8 years experience including:
Strong interpersonal skills & customer focus.
C++ Object orientated design development of multi -threaded applications on UNIX platforms ( Linux , LynxOS )
High Performance Supercomputing clusters.

Additional experience will be helpful in the following areas:
UNIX System Administration
UNIX Device Driver development
Hardware / software integration testing
Tcl / TK , Python, Bash Scripting
CVS
Avionic communication buses ( AFDX, ARINC - 429, CAN , MIL - STD - 1553 , etc)
