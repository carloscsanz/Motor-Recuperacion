When to Visit Bonito in Mato Grosso do Sul



You can visit Bonito year round, but some information are important when you plan your trip.

In June and July temperatures are lowest and can not attract those who do not like cold.

December, January and February are the months of high season there, the tourists are all over the place in the city and prices rise significantly.

In the remaining months of the year, the city is empty, prices fall and the waters are more transparent for the tours of fluctuation.

Getting to Northern Pantanal in Brazil



By Road

The access to North Pantantal is made from the capital Cuiabá.

To reach the city of Cáceres, the access is made by the road BR 070.

The way to Poconé is done by the road MT 080, which also gives access to Highway Transpantaneira.

The access to Barão de Melgaço is made from the Cuiabá road BR 070 towards São Vicente. Following the indicative signs, into a dirt road and follow approximately 70 km to get Barão de Melgaço.

Distances

Barão de Melgaço - Cuiabá: 80 km
Cáceres - Cuiabá: 220 km
Poconé - Cuiabá: 110 km

By Air

The main access is by the Marechal Rondon Airport in Cuiabá. The main airlines have flights from / to this airport.

You can book your hotel in Cuiabá on the website below.

Tourist Attractions in Guarda do Embaú



Guarda do Embaú is located in the state of Santa Catarina, in southern Brazil.

This small city offers hills, rivers and dunes of sand for tourists.

You will find many beaches with strong waves, ideal for surfers.

Most tourists are formed by young and simple people.

The place also offers limited infrastructure for tourism, with just a few inns and restaurants.

The beach called Guarda do Embaú is a haven for surfers.

This beach is only accessible through the Rio da Madre (Madre River) by foot or by small boat.
