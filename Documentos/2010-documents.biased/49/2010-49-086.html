Quote:
Could you please recommend some means for fast indexation?
1) Check your internal navigation for 404 errors and direct non-javascript links.

2) Submit your web-sites to google.com/addurl/

3) Register yourself on Google Webmaster Tools and submit a sitemap.
A simplest site-map is just a text file of links.

4) Get incoming links.

5) Remember Google likes changing content.
