Who founded facebook?

Mark Zuckerberg
Mark Elliot Zuckerberg (born May 14, 1984), the American computer programmer known for creating Facebook, an online social directory, with the help of Harvard roommates Chris Hughes and Dustin Moskovitz
wikipedia
Dustin Moskovitz
Dustin Aaron Moskovitz (born May 22, 1984 to Richard A. Moskovitz and Nancy Siegel) co-founded the online social directory, Facebook, with Harvard roommates Mark Zuckerberg and Chris Hughes
wikipedia
Chris Hughes
Chris Hughes (born on November 26, 1983) co-founded the online social directory, Facebook, in 2004 with Harvard roommates Mark Zuckerberg and Dustin Moskovitz
