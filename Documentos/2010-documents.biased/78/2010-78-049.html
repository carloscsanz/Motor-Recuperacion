Re: Where the heck did my site go?
Is your site still listed on google? site:yoursite.com

Does your site show when you search for your domain name + com? yoursite com

Did you make any major changes to your site such as Internal Linking? Content?

There are many reasons why a site will stop showing all of a sudden. Google has a lot of datacenters and their data is not synchronized. I wouldn't be too concerned. I've had a page stop showing and the next time it showed up a day or 2 later, it was actually ranking much higher.

Also, there are many tools you can use to check the ranking of your pages. I remember the days when I checked page by page but you need to become more efficient if you're serious about IM.
