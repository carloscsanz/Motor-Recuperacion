Bobarra Lady Lindy Laptop Bag - Laptop Bag of the Week

I had an easy time choosing my latest Laptop Bag of the Week - I can't get enough of this Lady Lindy laptop tote by Bobarra.

We just started carrying the Bobarra "Lady Lindy" line at CareerBags.com and they're all super cute. Great prints and a highly functional design make the Lady Lindy a must-have for you frequent fliers out there.

All the Lady Lindy prints are fun, but I was especially drawn to the Saigon blue and brown print here on the right.

I also love the scalloped edges at the top; and I REALLY love that the Lady Lindy is another checkpoint friendly laptop bag!

Inspired by the first lady of air travel (and one of my heroes) Amelia Earhart, the fashionable Lady Lindy comes in two sizes - 13" for smaller laptops and 15" for your more standard size computer.

Each Lady Lindy laptop tote has a padded compartment for your laptop, lots of space for your other electronic goodies and - this part of my favorite - a flip-out quart-sized plastic pouch to hold all of your small bottles of liquid.

I think this idea is genius - helps you get organized BEFORE getting to the airport and it saves on disposable ziploc bags at security. Everyone wins!

So before you jet off to summer destinations, be sure to check out the Bobarra Lady Lindy laptop totes at CareerBags.com!
